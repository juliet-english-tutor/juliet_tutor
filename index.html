Verb "to be" · present & past

A1 practice · three tasks: choose, fill, and rewrite

Task 1

choose the correct form
Select the right verb: am / is / are / was / were
I _____ a student now.
They _____ at home yesterday.
This book _____ interesting.
We _____ at the park last Monday.
My sister _____ very happy now.

Task 2

fill in: am / is / are / was / were
Write the correct form of "to be" (present or past).
She _____ a teacher now.
We _____ at the cinema last night.
I _____ tired yesterday.
You _____ my best friend.
The children _____ in the garden now.

Task 3

rewrite: present ↔ past
Change the sentence from present to past, or past to present.
Now: He is at school.
(yesterday)
Yesterday: They were in London.
(now)
Now: I am happy.
(last week)
Last summer: She was in Spain.
(now)
Now: We are at home.
(last night)
